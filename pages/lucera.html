Lucera Specific Rules

The continent of Lucera is a cold and unforgiving place. Scavaging for Food, keeping warm, and keeping your companions alive are essential for survival during your expedition. Please review the below rules that are specific to the lands of Lucera.

Survival

Cold Weather and Warmth Points

Lucera is cold. Very cold. The kind of cold that will steal your warmth from you, and when the wind blows it only gets worst.

You start with a number of Warmth Points (wp) equal to your CON score. During the day as you travel, hunt for food, or explore ruins, your warmth point decreases depending on the weather outside.

Calm or Light = -1 wp/hour
Moderate = -2 wp/hour
Severe = -4 wp/hour
Wet = -6 wp/hour, takes 3 hours to dry out

Once your character reaches 0 warmth points, your Speed is halved, and you have Disadvantage on ability checks that relay on CON/STR/DEX. you being to loose CON at the rate of -1/hour. If you reach 0 CON - you die.
You regain 1 CON per Long Rest.

Regaining Warmth Points

To regain Warmth Points, sit yourself down by a fire. The amount of Warmth Points gained per hour depends on the amount of firewood your burn per hour (in lbs).

Pounds of wood burned per hour:
2 lbs = +1 warmth point
3 lbs = +2 warmth points
4 lbs = +3 warmth points

Weather that is Moderate or above will require at least a windbreak in order to start the fire and gain benefit from it.

Shelter

Shelter will decrease the amount of Warmth Points lost per hour (but, by itself, never bring it above 0):
Canvas Tent = +1 Shallow cave = +1 Fur Tent = +2 Deep Cave/House/etc will always result in 0 wp lost per hour.

Other Modifiers

Quality winter gear will increase your pool of Warmth Points. A heavy fur cloak (8 lbs) for example, will increase your Warmth Points buy +5.

Being burned, while not fun or good for your overall health, will also increase your warmth points.

Examples

Collecting Firewood

A Strength check will be requied for harvesting firewood. For every number greater than the DC (base DC 10), you gain that many pounds of firewood.
For example, a Strength check of 22 would result in a net gain of 12 pounds of firewood.
One firewood check takes 1 hour.

Modifiers

The correct tool is important for efficient wood gathering.

Dagger, Flail, War Pick, or other ill-suited weapon = disadvantage on the Strength check.
Sword, Halberd, or equivalent bladed weapon = x1 wood gained.
Hand Axe or Battle Axe = x2 wood gained.
Wood Axe = x3 wood gained.

Gathering Food

Scavaging or Hunting for food is essential to keeping yourself fed in the cold snowy wastes.
A Survival check is required when foraging for food, for every number greater than the DC (DC varies by area), you gain that many pounds of food.
A Survival check of 22 on a DC18 check would net 4 pounds of food.
A Survival check for foraging food takes 1 hour.
2 pounds of food per day are needed to sustain your character.
If you are not profiecient in Survival, roll at disadvantage.

Other Sources of Food

There are other ways to stock up on your rations other than foraging. Fishing near the shore, hunting seal or walrus, or coming across a heard of caribou are all viable sources of obtaining food. The pounds of food obtained by hunting depends on the kill.
