Custom Rymura Packs

To start, select a pack from the dropdown list below. The colored rows correspond to the listed item categories.
For instance: “3 x Dungeoneering Gear” means that you can select three items from the corresponding “Dungeoneering Gear” table.

You do not need to pick these items right away: You may select an item at any time during your adventures.

For example: You’re in a deep underground complex and find yourself really needing a crowbar to open that stuck door. You turn one of your Dungeoneering Gear items into a crowbar and add it to your pack, it’s now assumed that you have always had a crowbar on your character. You still have two Dungeoneering item choices when needed.
