Character Creation Rules

Please review the below rules and restrictions prior to creating your character. All restrictions can be challenged and debated: Let me know if you see something you want to use that is restricted.

Character Creation

Stats

Roll for stats: 4d6dr1 or 3d6, or 27 Point Buy. If rolling for stats, please do so in the in-game chat.

Customization/flavoring is allowed and encouraged. If you want something extra just run it by the DM.

Please review the Campaign Rules in case it affects your character.

Character Info

You are free to play as nobility, but you will be (or at least start as) a very minor noble.

Feel free to name your hometown and describe their location.

Creation Questions that would be helpful to tie in your stories:
What is, or was, your profession (how do, or did, you make a living)?
Do you have any family? Where do they live and what do they do?
Does your character have any short and/or long-term goals?

Languages

So, you’re a smalltime human farmer from western Tarmentis that is just starting out adventuring, how would you possibly have learned Elvish, Dwarvish, or Orcish having never seen such creatures? If, for background or RP purposes, you are looking for an alternative, consider these languages:

The Trade Language

If you can’t think of a valid reason your character might be fluent in another language, and want an alternative, consider selecting The Trade Language. The Trade Language is used by Shop Keepers, Peddlers, buyers, and sellers of many peoples to bridge the gap between races. The Trade Language is not extensive, but it’s a good way to tell an Elf huntsman that doesn’t speak common that you want X amount of rabbits for X amount of copper.

Old Common

The current iteration of Common happened through years and years of evolution. However, many tomes in the old libraries, parchment found in chests, and madmen scrawling’s in caves are in Old Common. In addition, the old way to say a location might contain a clue or added information about that place. (Requirement: 12 INT or greater).

Ancient Dwarven

(The ‘Sylvan’ equivalent) Before the rise of humans, Elves ruled the land. However, the deep dark tunnels and caves of the world have always belonged to the dwarves. (Requirement: 12 INT or greater).

Thieves Cant

The unrepentant thief is not the only one that have a use for the secret language of the underworld. Perhaps you used to be a peddler, and sometimes deal with… items of suspect origin.

Equipment and Wealth

Adventuring Gear and Weight

All equipment must have a weight value.

When purchasing or using starting packs, break out each item and give it the appropriate weight value.

Wealth and Coin

Copper is the default currency of the world, and gold is hard to come by for common folk. You can see a list of professions and estimated earnings here, and purchasable gear and services here

1 Platinum = 500 copper

Ignore any starting gold you would get from your class, background, or any other source.

Starting wealth = (3d8+6 Silver (x2 Copper))*level. A roll of 19 = 19 Silver 38 Copper starting wealth. If you are starting at level 2, you would double that amount.

Race Changes and Restrictions

Inhabitants of civilization in Rymura consist primarily of humans, elves, dwarves, halflings, and gnomes. Other intelligent races are known to exist but have little contact with the civilized world. They are generally found in the wilder places of the continent.

*Roleplaying as one of these races will affect how people treat and react to you, the more “human/humanoid” you look, the more “normal” the reaction from the general populace. For instance, if you play a Hobgoblin and go into a northern village, you will most likely be met with fear and hostility. In the South you may be treated with wonder and disbelief. I am not trying to discourage you from playing a race, just be prepared from a roleplay point of view.*

Please review the below list when selecting a race, or subrace, for possible changes or restrictions. If you want to play a race, or use a feature that has been restricted, please let me know and I will work with you.

If the Race/Subrace you are taking is not in the PHB, XGE, SCAG, VGtM, or MToF, please okay it with the DM
